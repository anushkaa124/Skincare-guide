Common Questions

Clearing up the Confusion

How long until I see results? +
Skin turnover takes about 28 days. Retinol or Vitamin C may take 6–12 weeks.
Can I mix Vitamin C and Retinol? +
Use Vitamin C in the morning and Retinol at night.
Do I need sunscreen indoors? +
Yes, especially if you sit near windows.
What is correct order of products? +
General rule: Thinnest to thickest. Cleanser > Toner > Serums > Moisturizer > Oil > SPF (AM only).
Is natural skincare better? +
Not necessarily. "Natural" is unregulated. Many synthetic ingredients (like lab-made Hyaluronic Acid) are safer, more stable, and more sustainable than natural alternatives. Focus on formulation, not just origin.
